# How to Find DNS Settings in 123-reg

## Different Ways to Access DNS in 123-reg:

### Method 1: Through Control Panel

1. **Log into 123-reg:**
   - Go to https://www.123-reg.co.uk
   - Click **"Login"** (top right)
   - Enter your credentials

2. **Navigate to Domains:**
   - Look for **"My Account"** or **"Control Panel"**
   - Click on **"Domains"** or **"Domain Management"**
   - You should see a list of your domains

3. **Find Your Domain:**
   - Look for `medcare247.co.uk` in the list
   - You might see options like:
     - **"Manage"**
     - **"DNS"**
     - **"DNS Management"**
     - **"DNS Settings"**
     - **"Advanced DNS"**
     - **"Zone Editor"**
     - A gear icon (⚙️) or three dots (⋯)

### Method 2: Direct Links to Try

After logging in, try these direct URLs:
- https://www.123-reg.co.uk/controlpanel/domains
- https://www.123-reg.co.uk/controlpanel/domain-management

### Method 3: Look for These Terms

123-reg might use different terminology. Look for:
- **"DNS Management"**
- **"DNS Settings"**
- **"Advanced DNS"**
- **"DNS Records"**
- **"Zone Editor"**
- **"Name Server Management"** (but you DON'T want this - you want DNS records)

### Method 4: Contact 123-reg Support

If you can't find it:

1. **Live Chat:**
   - Look for **"Support"** or **"Help"** button
   - Click **"Live Chat"** or **"Contact Support"**
   - Ask: "How do I access DNS records for my domain?"

2. **Phone Support:**
   - 123-reg support number (usually on their website)
   - Ask them to guide you to DNS settings

3. **Email Support:**
   - Send them an email asking for help accessing DNS records

## What You're Looking For:

Once you find DNS settings, you should see:
- A list of existing DNS records (A, CNAME, MX, etc.)
- An **"Add Record"** or **"Create Record"** button
- Options to edit or delete existing records

## Alternative: Use 123-reg's Name Servers

If you can't find DNS settings, you might need to:
1. Change your domain's name servers to point to Vercel
2. But this is more complex - DNS records are easier

## Quick Checklist:

- [ ] Logged into 123-reg account
- [ ] Found "Domains" or "Domain Management" section
- [ ] Located `medcare247.co.uk` in your domain list
- [ ] Found "DNS", "DNS Settings", "Manage", or similar option
- [ ] Can see/add/edit DNS records
